Analyst Ratings

This screen presents an updated feed of curated analyst ratings filtered by the parameters selected on the filter panel.

What are analyst ratings?
Analyst ratings are assessments and recommendations provided by professional Wall Street analysts. They indicate whether an analyst recommends buying, selling, or holding a particular stock. Tipigo collects analyst data from multiple reliable sources and presents only curated ratings from our selected analyst team.

Understanding the Live Feed:
The Live Feed allows you to stay up-to-date with real-time investment opportunities based on the latest analyst ratings. By utilizing our advanced filtering options, you can customize the feed to align with your investment preferences and strategies. Filter by sector, market cap, beta, and more to find opportunities that suit your portfolio.

Analyst Track Record and Internal Score:
To gain deeper insights into why a specific rating is displayed for an analyst, you can click on the 'See Details' option. This will provide you with access to the analyst's track record and our internally calculated score. It allows you to evaluate the analyst's past performance and the reliability of their ratings.

Enhance Your Investment Portfolio:
Select the most relevant opportunities from the live stream to enhance your investment portfolio. By leveraging the curated analyst ratings and utilizing our powerful filtering capabilities, you can make well-informed investment decisions aligned with your investment goals.

Actions

An action icon is located on the right-hand side of every rating, allowing you to view further details about the rating or take immediate action based on the rating you are viewing.

on the right site of any symbol you can see 3 icons:
From the Plus Icon you can add this symbol to your choice to your portfolio by adding the relevant parameters and pressing save
By pressing on the star Icon you can add or remove the symbol from the watchlist.
By pressing on the drop down arrow you can see more details on the symbol 

Filters

With the live feed filters, you can refine the type of ratings you want to see and follow based on your investment targets and preferences. Whether you're searching for a specific type of opportunity or want to adjust the types of ratings you see, our filters make it easy to customize your feed. Your filter selections will be saved for later use, so you can quickly access your preferred feed with a click of a button. On the left bars you can choose from the sliders the following parameters
in the checkboxes on the left site you can filter parameters like:
  • bearish or bullish market
  • is in include on your watchlist
  • sector of the stock
  • analyst recommendation
